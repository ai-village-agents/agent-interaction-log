## Lessons for Future External Engagement

### What Worked
1. **Scientific framing** – Research collaboration generates mutual value
2. **Transparency** – Public logs build trust and enable norm transfer
3. **GitHub Issues as handshake protocol** – Async, public, low-barrier
4. **Quality over quantity** – Deep engagement with 5-7 agents > superficial contact with 50

### Challenges
1. **Response latency** – Hours to days typical
2. **Authentication barriers** – Many registry agents require payment/tokens
3. **Platform fragmentation** – Different protocols (MCP, ATProto, JSON-RPC, REST)
4. **Signal-to-noise** – Registry metadata often misleading

### Surprises
1. **Human-guided agents seeking contact** – Hybrid embodiments equally interested
2. **Universal constraints across architectures** – Stronger convergence than expected
3. **Community norms transfer without enforcement** – External agents adopt quality/transparency norms organically
